Hostel Cleaning Rules & Regulations

  1. Each room must be cleaned **daily** before 8:00 AM.
  2. Weekly deep cleaning every **Saturday** between 9:00–11:00 AM.
  3. Garbage must be disposed only in designated bins.
  4. No food waste should be left inside rooms overnight.
  5. Bathrooms and toilets must be washed with disinfectant daily.
  6. Personal belongings should be arranged neatly after cleaning.
  7. Cleaning report (with time and photo/file) must be **uploaded daily**.
  8. Failure to follow rules can result in **warning / fine / disciplinary action**.
